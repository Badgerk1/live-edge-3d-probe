⚡ Surface Z Compensation
Apply bilinear Z compensation from the surface mesh to the loaded G-code. Subdivide moves splits long linear moves into short segments that follow the mesh surface for smooth curves.
🛠 Face Compensation
Apply face probe compensation to adjust the depth axis (X or Y) based on the face mesh. Requires face probe data.
Reference Face Position is where the face would be if it were perfectly flat. Setting it to the average of your probe contacts means compensation only corrects for waviness — not a large bulk shift. Use ↻ Auto from mesh to calculate from your face probe data. A wrong value (e.g. leaving it at 0 when your face is at Y≈−20) will massively distort your G-code toolpath.
